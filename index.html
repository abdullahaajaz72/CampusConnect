Frequently Asked Questions

How do I use this Map?

You firstly enter the Destination of the place, then press the directions button and then enter the from location and the map guides you efficiently.

Will this be able to look for my current location?

Unfortunately no, this won't be able to detect your current location.

Is there an offline version of the map?

Right now, the map is an online-only feature. However, we plan to introduce offline capabilities in future updates for ease of use without an internet connection.

Do I need to be logged in?

No, you do not need to be logged in to use the map. It is open and accessible to all users.
